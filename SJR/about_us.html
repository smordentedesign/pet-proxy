About Us

With more love than resources, South Jersey Rescue began it's journey more than fifteen years ago. A 'small but mighty' core group of volunteers work strictly from our own, as well as foster homes, to diligently find safe, loving environments for all the animals entrusted to our care. Our journey has taken us as far north as upstate New York, and southern bound to rural West Virginia. South Jersey Rescue has come a long way in building a prideful relationship with our local community, as well as with our adoptive families. Our commitment to give our "furry friends" a second chance by building relationships built on love, trust, and respect is on going.
We invite you to travel along with us, as our journey continues...

Our Mission

South Jersey Rescue's mission is to eliminate animal suffering, promote the importance of the human-animal bond and improve the role of animals in the well-being of people. All healthy and adoptable companion animals that come through our doors are given as long as it takes for us to find them a home of their own, regardless of age, beauty, or infirmity. In addition, we provide medical care and treatment for our shelter guests who need a little something extra to make them adoptable.

Our Story

South Jersey Rescue was founded in 2001 by a group of devoted animal lovers looking to make a difference.

Volunteer and Donate

We are a charitable organization under IRS Code Section 501(c)(3) status. All donations are tax deductible and you will receive a receipt by return mail for all monetary donations. Your donations provide the animals in our care with food, litter, and quality medical care. Without your support, many of these animals would not survive, but with your financial help and the TLC from our volunteers and foster parents loving homes, these animals get a second chance to find loving homes. The animals are grateful for your support and the adoptive homes are too.

We're always looking for new volunteers to join our cause! Thank you for your interest in volunteering for South Jersey Rescue! We are proud to have the support of so many wonderful volunteers. These dedicated people put their concern for animal welfare into action by donating their time, talents and energy.
