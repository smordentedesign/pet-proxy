Volunteer Info

volunteer info

On Site Volunteering

Time Commitment:

Volunteers are required to attend an orientation.

New Volunteers:

Beginning September 1st, 2016 all volunteers will have the option to purchase a "Volunteer Starter Kit" for $10. The kit will contain an South Jersey Rescue Volunteer t-shirt and an ID badge. Volunteer t-shirts for $7 and ID badges for $5 are also available separately. This covers South Jersey Rescue's cost only. T-shirts are optional, ID badges are required and are to be worn by ALL volunteers while volunteering at South Jersey Rescue. ID badges identify volunteers by name and reflect the completed levels of training. Please bring cash or a check with you at the time of Orientation to purchase these items.

Current South Jersey Rescue volunteers:

Volunteers have the option of purchasing a volunteer tee shirt for $7. Replacement ID badges are $3. Volunteers MUST have an ID badge on while volunteering at South Jersey Rescue. E-mail volunteers at SJRNJ.org if you would like to purchase a volunteer shirt or need a replacement badge.

Children and on-site volunteering:

Those who are 16 or 17 years old may volunteer independently with written consent from a parent. Volunteers aged 12-15 may volunteer with a parent or guardian present at all times who also attended orientation. Children younger than 12 may become involved through Humane Education, attending our Summer Camp, or by soliciting donations in our Trash to Treasure program.

How to get started:

South Jersey Rescue has two applications available for volunteers that wish to volunteer on-site; one for Junior Volunteers and one for volunteers aged 16 years and up.

Special Event Volunteering

Volunteers assist in 4 main areas:

  • Fairs and festivals
  • Pet therapy
  • Tabling and small events
  • Third party fundraising

Large special events include our annual Paws and Feet 5k run and doggy fun day, Yappy Hour pet socials, our online auction and our 50/50 raffle.

How to get started as a community outreach volunteer:

Fill out the Community Outreach Volunteer Application.
