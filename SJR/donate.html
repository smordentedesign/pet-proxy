Items Needed for Our Shelter Pets & Clinic

SJR accepts donations and goods that significantly impact our ability to help homeless pets in our community.

Our Amazon Wishlist

If you prefer the convenience of choosing items and having them shipped directly to us, shop through Amazon's wish list.
Please note that Amazon does not provide us with your contact info, but we want to thank you for your donation! Please email SJRNJ.org with your mailing address.

We also accept donated items!

Items can be dropped off at our Adoption Center. If you need help, just let our office staff know. Drop off Tues- Fri from 12pm- 7pm or Sat/Sun/Mon from 12pm- 5pm.
