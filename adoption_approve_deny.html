Approval/Denial Instructions

Scroll through each section and check the adoption information carefully. Once you have reviewed and verified each section of the adoption application, you are required to provide an electronic signature (this is just your name typed) to show that the section has been checked and approved. Once all of the sections have been reviewed and approved, the adoption is able to be approved by clicking on the APPROVE button at the bottom of the page.

If you deem the provided information to not be sufficent and do not wish to approve a section, please do not provide an electronic signature. At this point you would need to contact the adoption applicant to work out the issue or if you choose, you may deny the adoption outright and the applicant will be notified automatically via email. To DENY an adoption, click the DENY button at the bottom of the page.
